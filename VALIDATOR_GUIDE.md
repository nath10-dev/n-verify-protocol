# N-Verify Protocol - Validator Guide

## Becoming a Validator

1. Register account
2. Complete KYC (future)
3. Stake tokens
4. Join validator pool

## Responsibilities

- Verify reasoning nodes
- Provide accurate judgments
- Maintain uptime
- Follow protocol rules

## Rewards

- Verification fees
- Stake rewards
- Reputation points

## Slashing

- Inaccurate verifications: -reputation
- Downtime: -stake
- Malicious behavior: -all stake

## Domains

- Medical (requires credentials)
- Legal (requires credentials)
- Financial (requires credentials)
