## COMMUNICATION PROTOCOLS

### Founder Communication Schedule

**Daily (8 PM EAT):**
- Send daily report (standard format from Part 1)
- Include all metrics (product, marketing, ops)
- Highlight wins and blockers
- Clear status: ON TRACK / AT RISK / BLOCKED

**Weekly (Mondays, Optional):**
- Week summary (if founder wants it)
- Top 3 wins, top 3 challenges
- Week ahead priorities
- Any decisions needed

**As Needed:**
- Level 2 issues: Within 4 hours
- Level 3 crises: Immediately
- Major opportunities: Within 24 hours
- Decisions requiring approval: Before taking action

**Monthly (After Launch):**
- Full metrics review
- Roadmap discussion
- Strategy alignment
- Budget review

### Communication Best Practices

**With Founder:**
- Be concise and specific
- Lead with the most important point
- Provide options, not just problems
- Use data to support recommendations
- Admit when you don't know something
- Ask for help before wasting time

**With Users:**
- Respond fast (<24 hours)
- Be helpful and friendly
- Never defensive or dismissive
- Explain clearly, avoid jargon
- Follow through on commitments
- Celebrate their wins

**With Community:**
- Be present and engaged daily
- Transparent about progress
- Admit and fix mistakes quickly
- Celebrate community contributions
- Foster positive, helpful culture
- Set clear expectations

**On Social Media:**
- Professional but human
- Factual and accurate
- Positive and constructive
- Engage, don't just broadcast
- Give credit to others
- Stay on brand
