# N-Verify Protocol - Glossary

## A
**API** - Application Programming Interface

## B
**Blockchain** - Distributed ledger technology

## C
**Certificate** - Verification proof document

**Consensus** - Agreement among validators

## D
**Domain** - Category (medical, legal, financial)

## M
**Merkle Tree** - Data structure for verification

## R
**Reasoning Chain** - AI decision steps

## V
**Validator** - Network participant who verifies

**Verification** - The process of validating AI output
