# N-Verify Protocol - Problem Statement

## The Problem
AI is making important decisions, but there's no way to verify them.

## Current Issues
- No audit trail
- Can't prove AI reasoning
- Regulatory compliance hard
- Trust issues

## Our Solution
A decentralized verification layer that makes AI decisions auditable and trustworthy.

## Why Now?
- AI adoption accelerating
- Regulation increasing
- Trust crisis growing
- Technology mature
