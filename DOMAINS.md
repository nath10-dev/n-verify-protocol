# N-Verify Protocol - Domain Details

## Medical Domain

### Validators
- Medical doctors
- Healthcare professionals
- Medical researchers

### Knowledge Sources
- PubMed
- UMLS
- RxNorm
- SNOMED CT
- Clinical guidelines

### Verification Criteria
- Factual accuracy of medical claims
- Drug interaction warnings
- Diagnosis accuracy
- Treatment recommendations

## Legal Domain

### Validators
- Lawyers
- Legal scholars
- Paralegals

### Knowledge Sources
- Legal precedent databases
- Case law
- Statutory law
- Regulatory documents

### Verification Criteria
- Legal citation accuracy
- Precedent relevance
- Statutory interpretation

## Financial Domain

### Validators
- Financial analysts
- CPAs
- Investment professionals

### Knowledge Sources
- SEC filings
- Financial databases
- Market data
- Regulatory reports

### Verification Criteria
- Financial data accuracy
- Risk assessment validity
- Investment recommendation soundness
