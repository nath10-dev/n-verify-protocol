# N-Verify Protocol - FAQ

## General

### What is N-Verify Protocol?
N-Verify is a decentralized verification layer that transforms AI model outputs into cryptographically auditable reasoning chains.

### Why do I need AI verification?
AI systems in healthcare, legal, and financial sectors require compliance with regulations like EU AI Act. N-Verify provides proof of AI reasoning.

### How does it work?
1. Submit AI output for verification
2. We extract the reasoning chain
3. Validators verify each step
4. Consensus is calculated
5. Certificate is generated and stored on-chain

## Technical

### What blockchains are supported?
Ethereum (Sepolia testnet → Mainnet)

### How long does verification take?
Under 5 seconds for the MVP

### What domains are supported?
Medical, Legal, and Financial

### Is my data secure?
Yes. Data is encrypted and stored on IPFS with cryptographic proofs.
