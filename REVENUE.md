# N-Verify Protocol - Revenue Model

## Revenue Streams

1. **Verification Fees**
   - Per verification: $0.10 - $1.00
   - Based on domain and complexity
   
2. **Subscription Plans**
   - Free: 10 verifications/month
   - Starter: $29/month - 100 verifications
   - Pro: $99/month - 1000 verifications
   - Enterprise: Custom pricing

3. **Validator Staking**
   - Validator deposits stake
   - Earn fees from verifications
   
4. **Enterprise Services**
   - Custom integrations
   - Dedicated support
   - SLA guarantees

## Pricing Examples

| Plan | Price | Verifications | Features |
|------|-------|---------------|----------|
| Free | $0 | 10/mo | Basic |
| Starter | $29 | 100/mo | API, Email |
| Pro | $99 | 1000/mo | Priority, Webhooks |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited | Everything |

## Cost Structure

- OpenAI API: ~50% of costs
- Infrastructure: ~30%
- Support: ~10%
- Profit: ~10%
