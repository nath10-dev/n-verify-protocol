# N-Verify Protocol - FAQ

## What is N-Verify?
A decentralized verification layer for AI reasoning chains.

## How does it work?
1. Submit AI output
2. Extract reasoning
3. Validate with validators
4. Generate certificate
5. Store on blockchain

## What domains?
Medical, Legal, Financial (more coming)

## How much does it cost?
Free tier available, Pro from $49/month

## Is it secure?
Yes! Encryption, audits, compliance

## How long does verification take?
Seconds to minutes depending on complexity

## Can I become a validator?
Yes! Stake tokens and earn rewards
