# N-Verify Protocol - One Page

N-Verify Protocol: Decentralized AI verification layer

## What We Do
Verify AI reasoning chains with cryptographic proofs

## Why It Matters
AI decisions need audit trails in healthcare, legal, financial

## How It Works
1. Submit AI output
2. Extract reasoning
3. Validate with experts
4. Generate certificate
5. Store on blockchain

## Features
- Medical, legal, financial domains
- <5 second verification
- Blockchain certificates
- Distributed validators
